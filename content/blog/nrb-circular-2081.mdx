# 5 Things CAs Need to Know About Today's NRB Circular

The Nepal Rastra Bank (NRB) has issued a new circular that introduces several key changes to forex reporting requirements. Here's what you need to know.

## 1. New Reporting Timeline

Financial institutions must now submit forex transaction reports within 48 hours, down from the previous 7-day window.

## 2. Enhanced KYC Requirements

All forex transactions above NRs 500,000 now require enhanced due diligence documentation.

## 3. Digital Reporting Mandate

Paper-based reporting is no longer accepted. All submissions must be through the NRB's digital portal.

## 4. Penalty Structure Changes

Late reporting penalties have been increased from 0.5% to 1.5% of the transaction value.

## 5. Compliance Officer Requirement

Banks with forex trading volumes exceeding NRs 1 billion annually must designate a dedicated compliance officer.
